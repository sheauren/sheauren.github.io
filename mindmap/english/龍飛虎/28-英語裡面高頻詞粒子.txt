    重點
        -ing

    高頻詞粒子
        固定用法代表特定含義
        -ing
            正在
            eating
            playing
            going
        -ed
            了
            played
            worked
            finished
            盡量用畫面去感覺
                聽到就有畫面
                就不用翻譯
        -er
            更
            Richer
            Faster
            Slower
            Poorer
            常在片語使用
                -er than
                比...更
                richer than
                faster than
                slower than
                poorer than
        -est
            最
            richest
            fastest
            slowest
            poorest
